1

Goal

2020 Casting established a new way of working that made a huge impact on the film industry and proudly was the first agency to take digital photographs of all its artistes. Many advances in technology have been made since then but we have always remained at the forefront and were also the first to use text messaging systems to contact our artistes before it was even commercially available. The currently developed back-office application has a substantial complexity in its management and is comprised of several areas. The following were the most important challenges faced:

  1. To complete an internal financial system which required a custom workflow to be implemented matching the characteristics of the business model.

  2. To increment the number of user registrations.

  3. To account for internal workload analytics for people involved in the process.

  4. To save time in communicating selected artistes to customers.

  5. To simplify the current booking workflow and information tracking.

2

Process

At the beginning, we were sure to have a deep understanding of the business model and current way of operation of the business to make sure we had a solid base upon to think an adequate solution. We spent some time defining key concepts and even writing the business operation ourselves to make sure we understood precisely the challenge we were facing. Once all of this was done, we got to designing a solution which encompassed the workflow and which also took into account potential errors and mistakes that could be made along the journey to allow for rollbacking certain processes and make them open to corrections.

For incrementing the number of registered users we took base measures of the current operation standards and reviewed the way in which this process was being made. Eventually, we decided it was best to completely rethink the registration process to make it more streamlined. A great deal of data was needed to create the user accounts and because of the nature of the business it was actually required. A moderation system was put in place to also have the ability to check registrants.

Most of the workload analytics were actually taken from the database itself, but in addition we integrated Keen.io as another form of querying the data to allow for a simple way to allow customized reports quickly.

Quickly communicating artiste selection to customers is of vital importance for moving projects forward so we had to rethink the way this was done, which was mainly using PDFs that the application generated, we talked through with our customer over alternatives and a more modern approach to information sharing which allowed us to come up with a nicer solution.

Currently our work continues with 2020 casting and we intent to upgrade other modules of the application to increase productivity and ease of employee onboarding and training by simplifying the booking workflow, which has been continuously delivered while real employess test drive it.

3

Impact

2020 Casting adopted the platform as the main point of control for essential business activities and the financial module quickly overtook the previous accounting books based approach they were using, making it far easier to manage its balances.

The user registration process was streamlined to account for a completely web based application process which allowed people to pause their registration at any point and come back later. The active user base doubled in a matter of a couple of months, now reaching nearly 20K users (as of June 2017).

The workload analytics implementation using Keen.io let's administrators create their own filters and queries for computing efficiency and turnover over specific projects, people and time span.

Customers now expect interactive books for artiste selection instead of PDFs sent by email. The time it takes to communicate back and forth selection decisions has been cut almost tenfold.

The redesigned workflow simplifies communication between users and internal staff, as well as organizing the work into more manageable chunks and complete history listing of actions made, which greatly minimizes errors and misunderstandings when having to coordinate large crowds.
