London Ruby Unconference

Join our october 2017 event!

Connecting the London Ruby community!

The unconference is a gathering which aims to provide an open and creative conversation on topics related to Ruby to surface their collective knowledge, share ideas and insights. It is not like a normal conference; there is no pre-planned agenda and no fixed speakers. The Ruby Unconference is the perfect place for open-source contributors to gain traction and gather feedback on their projects.
